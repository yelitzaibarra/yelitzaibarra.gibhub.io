Kobe Bryant- American Basketball Player

Always a Hero in Our Hearts

We all somehow know of or about The famous Kobe Bryant. Maybe you've thrown a crumbled paper to the trash and said "KOBE" while you were at it. But that tradition now lays in our memory. Now instead of saying "KOBE" we say "FOR KOBE". The death of his and his daughted Gigi devastated everyone. Wheather you were his fan or not, you cant help but feel some type of empathy and orsympathy for the bryant family that changed and marked a special place in our childhoods forever.

Timeline
  • 1978- Kobe is born.
  • 1996- Lakers trade Vlade Divac to Charlotte Hornets for the draft rights to 17-year-old Bryant, the 13th pick in the NBA draft.
  • 1999-Bryant wins his first NBA title as the Lakers beat Pacers in six games.
  • 2001- He gets married to Vanessa Bryant.
  • 2003-Bryant is accused of sexual assult. He has his first kid.
  • 2012- Bryant makes 15th All-Star team
  • 2020- Bryant and his daughter Gigi lose their lives in a plane crash in Calabasas, CA.
